I worked as an Early Childhood Educator in Toronto, making my way teaching through various age groups. The most challenging being toddlers. Designing experiences that allow children to thrive was crucial to the success of my daily routine. Offering activities that are too advanced and open ended runs the risk of disaster - paint everywhere, sparkles everywhere. This inherently exercised my skill of strategy, creativity, and surprisingly - restraint. As a UX Designer and strategist I hope to influence the educational sector. I have worked with children for years as an educator in Toronto and Berlin and want to extend my pedagogical and developmental craft to create meaningful interactive experiences for children.

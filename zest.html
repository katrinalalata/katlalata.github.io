Share Your Food.
This app is intended for users to save and organize recipes created by the user or saved from another source. Users would be able to add ingredients, instructions, footnotes, organize with keywords, search with keywords such as ingredients or meal type, send to other users or to contacts, add to pinterest, and rate recipes. This app is intended to be a personal and shareable recipe database. I was responsible in the discovery, strategy, information architecture, visual design and development. Google Forms was used to conduct and track the survey process, as well as Figma for the prototypes, branding and style guides.
